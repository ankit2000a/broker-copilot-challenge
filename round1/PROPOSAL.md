# Round 1 Proposal

## Introduction
This proposal outlines the objectives and methodologies for Round 1 of the Broker Copilot Challenge.

## Objectives
1. To evaluate the key requirements of the broker's operations.
2. To develop a prototype that meets performance standards.
3. To gather feedback from stakeholders during the review phase.

## Methodology
- Conduct interviews with domain experts.
- Analyze existing systems and processes.
- Develop a minimum viable product (MVP) based on findings.

## Conclusion
Effective execution of this proposal will lead to significant advancements in the Broker Copilot project, setting a strong foundation for subsequent rounds.
